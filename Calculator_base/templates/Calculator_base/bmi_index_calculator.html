BMI Index Calculator

Instructions:

  • This is a Body-Mass-Index Calculator.
  • Given are the empty fields for Weight(in kg) and Height(in feet).
  • Enter the values in the empty fields according to their respective labels.
  • Weight must not be less than or equal to 0. Same goes for Height.
  • If the Index shows 'Invalid', please check whether the enterd values satisfies the conditions.
  • For BMI < 18.5, Status = Underweight (Tip : You need to put on more weight.)
  • For BMI > 18.5 and < 25, Status = Normal (Tip : Your BMI is perfectly fine.)
  • For BMI > 25 and < 30, Status = Overweight (Tip :It is important that you lose some amount of weight in order to improve your health.)
  • For BMI > 30 Status = Obese (Tip : You have way too much body fat in relation to your height. You must drop some weight.)
  • None of the status applies for Children. Healthy weight ranges change as the height of the child increases.
  • Please report for any issue.
